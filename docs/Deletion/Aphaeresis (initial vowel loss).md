Aphaeresis is a sound change that deletes the initial vowel in a word.  
  
One example of sporadic aphaeresis:  
  
Latin, Spanish  
*apoteca > bodega*  
  
Spanish has many examples of sporadic aphaeresis, such as:  
  
Latin, Spanish  
*acaso > caso*   
*episcopal > piscopal*  
*ahorita > horita*
